About DCUtv

An introduction from our TV Managers Shane & Donal

Welcome to DCUTV! We’re the home for all things film and video production in the Media Production Society. We do everything type of video from society-wide video productions, weekly videos on our DCUMPS YouTube channel and VLOGS!!!!
We have raised money for numerous charities throughout our years through our fundraising efforts, from the 24 hour Broadcast to our pub quiz nights.
Through weekly events we have managed to create a supportive community and a media family within DCU where people can come together and make the highest quality of student media in Ireland.
We’ve got the camera’s, you bring the inspiration!
